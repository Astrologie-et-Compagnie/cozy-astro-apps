Welcome

Discover your Cosmic Leadership Style

This guided workbook helps you decode five key placements in your natal chart and turn them into a practical leadership tool for your life and business.

You'll explore three modules — your Leadership Signature, your Decision-Making Style, and your Energy Budget — and end with a personalized Cosmic Leadership Profile you can download and keep.

What you'll need: your natal chart. If you don't have one yet, generate it for free in 2 minutes on astro-seek.com. You'll need your birth date, exact birth time, and birth location.

This is not a quiz — it's a reflection journey. Take your time with each step.

Placement 1 of 5

☀️ Your Sun sign & house

Your Sun is your core identity and life purpose — the energy you're here to radiate. In leadership, it's your central motivation: the "why" behind everything you build.

Placement 2 of 5

↑ Your Ascendant (Rising sign)

Your Ascendant is how you naturally show up — your presence, style, and first impression. Where your Sun is your inner driver, your Rising is your outer expression as a leader.

Placement 3 of 5

🌙 Your Moon sign & house

Your Moon governs your emotional world and inner security. In leadership, emotions aren't separate from decisions — they are the foundation of aligned decisions.

Placement 4 of 5

☿ Your Mercury sign & house

Your Mercury shapes how you think, process information, and communicate. It defines your decision rhythm — how you gather data, form opinions, and express your vision as a leader.

Placement 5 of 5

♂ Your Mars sign & house

Your Mars is your action principle — your drive, initiative style, and energy signature. It reveals how you take action, what fuels you, and how to manage your energy sustainably.

Module 1 — Leadership Signature

Sun + Ascendant: How do you lead?

Your Sun is what you're here to express. Your Ascendant is how you naturally show up. Together they form your leadership signature — the unique way you step into rooms, inspire others, and build your presence.

Reflect on these questions:

  • What do people often say about your energy or presence?
  • When do you feel most like yourself as a leader?
  • What kind of authority feels natural — not forced — to you?

Now write your Leadership Signature sentence in your own words. Short and imperfect is perfect.

Module 2 — Decision-Making Style

Moon + Mercury: How do you decide?

Your Moon tells you what you need to feel safe before deciding. Your Mercury tells you how you process information. Aligned decisions happen when both are honored — not bypassed.

Reflect on these questions:

  • Do you decide better alone or after talking it through?
  • What happens in your body when a decision feels right?
  • What conditions help you think most clearly?

Write your Decision-Making Style sentence in your own words.

Module 3 — Energy Budget

Mars: How do you take action?

Your Mars reveals your natural action style — not the productivity system you think you should follow, but the one that actually works for your energy. Cozy is not lazy. Aligned action is still action.

Reflect on these questions:

  • When do you feel most energized and in flow?
  • What kinds of tasks drain you faster than others?
  • How do you recharge between efforts?

Write your Energy Budget sentence in your own words.
