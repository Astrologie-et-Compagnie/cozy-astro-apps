Astro-CEO Cosmic Leadership Guide

Your cosmic leadership, made practical

Welcome

Discover your Cosmic Leadership Style

This guided workbook helps you decode five key placements in your natal chart and turn them into a practical leadership tool for your life and business.

You'll explore three modules — your Leadership Signature, your Decision-Making Style, and your Energy Budget — and end with a personalized Cosmic Leadership Profile you can download and keep.

What you'll need: your natal chart. If you don't have one, you can generate it for free in 2 minutes on astro-seek.com — you'll need your birth date, exact birth time, and birth location.

This is not a quiz — it's a reflection journey. Take your time with each step.

Placement 1 of 5

Your Sun sign & house

Your Sun represents your core identity, your life purpose, and what you're here to express in the world. In leadership, it's your natural drive — the "why" behind everything you build.

Placement 2 of 5

Your Ascendant (Rising sign)

Your Ascendant is how you show up in the world — your leadership presence, first impression, and natural style. Where your Sun is your inner driver, your Rising is your outer expression.

Placement 3 of 5

Your Moon sign & house

Your Moon represents your emotional world, inner security, and how you process feelings. In leadership and business, emotions are not separate from decisions — they are the foundation of aligned decisions.

Placement 4 of 5

Your Mercury sign & house

Your Mercury governs how you think, communicate, and process information. It shapes your decision rhythm — how you gather data, form opinions, and express your ideas as a leader.

Placement 5 of 5

Your Mars sign & house

Your Mars is your action principle — your drive, motivation style, and energy signature. It reveals how you take initiative, what fuels you, and how you manage your energy as a leader and business owner.

Module 1 — Leadership Signature

Sun + Ascendant: How do you lead?

Your Sun is what you're here to express. Your Ascendant is how you naturally show up. Together, they form your leadership signature — the unique way you step into rooms, inspire others, and build your brand.

Take a moment to reflect:

  • What do people often say about your presence or energy?
  • When do you feel most like yourself as a leader?
  • What kind of authority feels natural — not forced — to you?

Now, in your own words — even if it's short or imperfect — write your Leadership Signature sentence. This must come from you.

Module 2 — Decision-Making Style

Moon + Mercury: How do you decide?

Your Moon tells you what you need to feel safe before deciding. Your Mercury tells you how you process information. Aligned decisions happen when both are honored — not bypassed.

Take a moment to reflect:

  • Do you decide better alone or after talking it through?
  • What happens in your body when a decision feels right?
  • What conditions help you think most clearly?

Write your Decision-Making Style sentence in your own words.

Module 3 — Energy Budget

Mars: How do you take action?

Your Mars reveals your natural action style — not the productivity system you think you should follow, but the one that actually works for your energy. Cozy is not lazy. Aligned action is still action.

Take a moment to reflect:

  • When do you feel most energized and in flow?
  • What kinds of tasks drain you faster than others?
  • How do you recharge between efforts?

Write your Energy Budget sentence in your own words.
